Bio

Anirudh was born in Bengaluru (Karnataka), grew up in Solapur (Maharashtra) and is staying in Indore (Madhya Pradesh). He spent the first half of his life in Solapur, where he did Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from Walchand Insitute of Technology. Currently, he has submitted his PhD thesis at the Indian Institute of Technology Indore and is awaiting for the review. He has also worked as a CAD-CAE-CFD Consultant in a notable firm at Solapur. He is a husband, a friend, and a relentless researcher.
